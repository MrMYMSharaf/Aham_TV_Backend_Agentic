📅 Date: 15/05/2026
📌 $150m Twin Tower Project Launched

Home Lands Group has signed a Sales and Purchase Agreement with CHEC Port City Colombo to develop a twin tower high-rise project in Port City Colombo. The project, valued at $150 million, is the largest real estate investment by a single Sri Lankan developer. The twin towers will be located in the Central Park District, overlooking 40 acres of central parkland.
